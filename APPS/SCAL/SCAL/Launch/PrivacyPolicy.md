# Privacy Policy for SCAL - Smart Calorie Analysis Lens

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## Introduction

SCAL ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address and name (optional)
- **Health Data**: Nutrition goals, dietary preferences, and restrictions
- **Meal Data**: Foods you scan and log, meal times, and notes

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: App features used, frequency of use, and interaction patterns
- **Device Information**: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers
- **Performance Data**: Crash logs and performance metrics (anonymized)

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT store your photos on our servers
- We do NOT access your camera roll
- We do NOT share your health data with third parties
- We do NOT sell your personal information

## How We Use Your Information

### To Provide Services
- Process food images for nutritional analysis
- Track your nutrition history
- Provide personalized recommendations
- Sync data across your devices

### To Improve Our Service
- Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
- Improve food recognition accuracy
- Develop new features
- Fix bugs and improve performance

### To Communicate
- Send important app updates
- Provide customer support
- Send optional nutrition insights (with consent)

## Data Processing

### On-Device Processing
Whenever possible, we process data directly on your device:
- Basic food recognition using Core ML
- Nutrition calculations
- Data caching

### Cloud Processing
For advanced features, we may process data on secure servers:
- Complex multi-food detection
- AI coaching responses
- Meal plan generation

**Important**: Images are processed in real-time and immediately deleted from our servers. We do not store your photos.

## Data Storage & Security

### Where We Store Data
- Primary data stored locally on your device
- Backup data stored in your personal iCloud account
- Minimal account data on secure AWS servers (US-East region)

### Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- TLS 1.3 for all network communications
- Regular security audits
- Strict access controls
- Automatic data expiration

## Your Rights & Controls

### You Can Always:
- **Access Your Data**: Export all your data anytime
- **Delete Your Data**: Permanently delete your account and all data
- **Modify Your Data**: Edit or delete any meal entries
- **Control Processing**: Disable cloud features and use offline mode
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics and marketing communications

### Data Retention
- Active account data: Retained while account is active
- Deleted account data: Permanently removed within 30 days
- Anonymized analytics: Retained for up to 2 years
- Marketing data: Deleted immediately upon opt-out

## Third-Party Services

### We Use These Services:
- **Apple CloudKit**: For secure data sync (your data stays in your iCloud)
- **RevenueCat**: For subscription management (payment processing only)
- **Firebase Crashlytics**: For crash reporting (anonymized)
- **USDA FoodData**: For nutrition information (no personal data shared)

### We Do NOT Use:
- Social media tracking
- Advertising networks
- Data brokers
- Cross-app tracking

## Children's Privacy

SCAL is designed for users 4+ years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents can review and delete their child's information by contacting us.

## International Users

### GDPR Compliance (EU Users)
- Legal basis: Legitimate interests and consent
- You have rights to access, rectify, erase, and port your data
- Contact our Data Protection Officer: privacy@scalapp.com

### CCPA Compliance (California Users)
- You have the right to know what data we collect
- You can request deletion of your personal information
- We do not sell personal information
- No discrimination for exercising privacy rights
