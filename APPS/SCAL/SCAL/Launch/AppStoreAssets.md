## Review Guidelines Response

### 1. Health & Medical Information
SCAL provides nutritional information for educational purposes only. We clearly state in the app that users should consult healthcare professionals for medical advice. Nutritional data comes from USDA database and established food databases.

### 2. User-Generated Content
Users can only log their own meals. No social sharing features in v1.0. All content is private to the user.

### 3. Privacy Policy
We collect minimal data required for app functionality. No personal health data is shared with third parties. Users can delete all data at any time. Full privacy policy: https://scalapp.com/privacy

### 4. Subscriptions
- Clear pricing displayed before purchase
- Easy subscription management
- Cancel anytime from Settings
- Free tier always available
- Restore purchases supported

### 5. AI/ML Usage
We use on-device ML models when possible. Cloud processing is used for advanced features with user consent. No user photos are stored on our servers.
